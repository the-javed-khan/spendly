Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use the Service, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Service — to display your expense history, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your account. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your information from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. No personal or financial data is shared with any third party. We do not integrate analytics tools, advertising networks, or social media trackers into the Service.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.

Reach us at support@spendly.app.
